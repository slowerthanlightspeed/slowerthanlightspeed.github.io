What would possibly make an average cop more dangerous than an average civilian?

  • Cops are strapped whenever on duty! (side-pistol, tazer, shotgun, ...); civilians rarely carry.
  • Cops know that they can shoot civilians and get away with it most of the time; civilians know that, if they shot a cop, they will almost certainly go to jail (and have a bad time there... if they even live to see the jail!).
  • The Stanford Prison Experiment
  • Cops have back-up helicopters, swat units, k-9 units, and a central hub for coordinating their efforts.
